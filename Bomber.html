About the Game

Bomber is an exciting multiplayer online game where you and up to 4 friends can blast through chaotic, fast-paced matches. Fully customize your character with unique skins, create your own levels, and enjoy fun local voiceovers with an Egyptian twist!

  • 🧠 Level creator – design and play your own maps
  • 🎨 Unlockable skins and cosmetic customizations
  • 🏠 Interactive lobby while waiting for friends
  • 🎧 Egyptian-style voice lines for fun and immersion
