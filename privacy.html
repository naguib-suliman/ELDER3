Privacy Policy for Bomber & Most Games

Last updated: June 20, 2025

Bomber is developed and published by Elder-3 Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data when you use our game.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. However, when you log in using an Epic Games Account, certain information may be provided to us via Epic Online Services, such as:

  • Epic Account ID
  • Display Name
  • Basic profile data
How We Use Your Information

We use the information provided by Epic only to authenticate users and enable multiplayer features. We do not store or share this data with third parties.
