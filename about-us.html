Every real answer

When will the game be released?

We’re working hard to launch the game as soon as possible, but our priority is quality. We’ll announce an official release date once we’re confident the game is polished and bug-free. Follow us on social media for updates!

Will the game be available on specific platform? (PC/PlayStation/Xbox/Switch)

Right now, we’re focusing on launching the game on primary platform, e.g., PC first. then we’d love to bring it to other platforms—your support can make that happen!

What makes your game different from others in the genre?

What sets us apart is the unique fusion of visceral, high-octane Hack & Slash combat with deep, original lore rooted in our rich Egyptian heritage. While many games in the genre focus solely on action, we leverage the power of Unreal Engine 5 to deliver next-gen visual fidelity and an immersive world full of untold myths. We aren't just making an action game; we are crafting a culturally rich, premium RPG experience that feels fresh and visually breathtaking at middle east and worldwide.

Are you planning post-launch content (DLC or free updates)?

Yes, absolutely. We view our launch in late 2026 as just the beginning of the journey. We are fully committed to expanding the universe with a major DLC expansion planned for early 2027. In addition to that, we will provide ongoing support through updates to refine gameplay, ensure optimal performance, and keep our community engaged.
